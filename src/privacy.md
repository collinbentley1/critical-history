# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and shared when you visit the Critical History Map.

## Information Collection

The site does not provide public account creation and does not use Netlify Identity, Netlify CMS, or Google Analytics.

When you visit the site, hosting and infrastructure providers may process basic request information such as IP address, browser type, referring page, requested URL, and request time. This information is used to operate the site, monitor reliability, and screen for abuse.

The interactive map uses Mapbox. Mapbox may receive request information needed to serve map styles, tiles, and related map assets. The Add Location and Contact buttons open Typeform in a new tab; Typeform receives information only when you choose to open or submit that form.

## Third-Party Services

* [Google Cloud](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* [Mapbox](https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy)
* [Typeform](https://admin.typeform.com/to/dwk6gt)

## Information Use

Request information is used to operate and secure the site. Form submissions, if you choose to send one through Typeform, are used to review location suggestions or contact requests.

## Sharing

Information may be shared with service providers as described above. Information may also be shared to comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests, or protect rights and safety.

## Do Not Track

The site does not change behavior in response to Do Not Track signals.

## Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect operational, legal, or service-provider changes.
